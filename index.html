Privacy Policy


Effective Date: 18 January 2025


Your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application (“App”). If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at support@yekworks.com.


1. What Data We Collect and Use


a) Data You Upload:


• We process only the data you upload into the App. (Any data you choose to upload (i.e. face data, photo)) These datas are processed to provide the app’s functionality and are not stored on our servers. Once the processing is complete, the data is immediately discarded. We do not retain any data including face datas. Service providers we use also do not retain any data. They can retain data you upload for a couple of hours. We encourage you to see their privacy policy in "Third-Party APIs and Services" section


b) Crash and Performance Data:


• We use Crashlytics and similar tools to collect anonymized data about app crashes and performance issues. This helps us identify and fix problems to improve the App’s stability.

• The collected data may include device information (e.g., model, operating system), app version, and usage details at the time of the crash. This information is anonymized and does not include personal data.


c) Usage Analytics:


• We use Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics or similar tools) to gather information about how users interact with the App. This data helps us improve the user experience by understanding which features are most used and identifying potential issues.

• Collected information may include:

• Device type and operating system.

• App usage patterns (e.g., session duration, features accessed).

• General location data (city-level, non-specific).


2. Third-Party APIs and Services


Our app integrates with third-party services, including APIs and analytics tools. These services may collect or process data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review these policies to understand their practices:

• Crashlytics (Firebase): https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

• Google Analytics: https://policies.google.com/privacy

• fal.ai: https://fal.ai/privacy

    • Replicate: https://replicate.com/privacy


3. How We Protect Your Data


We implement strict technical measures to ensure the security of your data during processing. Specific protections include:

• End-to-end encryption for uploaded data during processing.

• Regular monitoring of our systems to prevent unauthorized access.


4. How We Use Your Data


We use the collected data for the following purposes:

• To process the data you upload and provide app functionality.

• To identify and fix app crashes or performance issues.

• To analyze app usage trends and improve features.


We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.


5. Your Privacy Rights


Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

• Access and Portability: Request access to usage data collected by analytics tools.

• Opt-Out: Disable analytics tracking through your device settings.


6. Data Retention


• Data: No datas are retained; they are discarded immediately after processing.

• Crash and Analytics Data: Retained for a reasonable period to improve app performance and comply with legal obligations.
